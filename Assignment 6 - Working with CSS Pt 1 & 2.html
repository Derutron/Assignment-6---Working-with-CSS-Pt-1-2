Jollof Rice Recipe

Ingredients List | Cooking Instructions | Photo of Finished Dish

Ingredients List

  • Item 1
  • 2 Cups of Rice
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper / Tatashe
  • 500ml Chopped Tomatoes
  • 100g Tomato Purée
  • 2 Scotch Bonnet
  • 100ml Vegetable Oil
  • Maggi
  • Salt to Taste
  • 1 Teaspoon Curry
  • 1 Teaspoon Thyme
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Garlic
  • 1/4 Teaspoon Ginger
  • 2 Cups Water or Stock

Cooking Instructions

1. Blend your tomatoes, scotch bonnet, and tatashe together.

2. Put your rice into a bowl and soak with hot water.

3. Heat up your oil.

4. Add your chopped onions and allow them to fry.

5. Add and fry your blended ingredients and purée.

6. Add your maggi, garlic, ginger, curry, thyme and salt.

7. Pour your rice into the pot and mix together.

8. Cook on low medium heat.

9. Cook until soft.

10. Serve the rice.
